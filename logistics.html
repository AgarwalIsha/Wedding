Logistics & Stay


  • For guests arriving at Kolkata airport & requiring pick up, please mail your tickets with subject line "KOLKATA_AIRPORT <DATE & TIME> PICK UP PLEASE.
  • Bookings have been arranged from 9/12 till 11/12. Check out time would be around 10 am. You may book Uber or Ola according to your convenience to go back to the airport.

  • Couples: Please pack your items in separate suitcases as you may not be allocated to the same room
  • Some people staying at the resort may be reallocated to rooms in a hotel near the resort on the night of December 10th. You will be notified in due course of time. Please prepare accordingly.
