Logistics



  • For guests arriving at the airport & needing pick up, please mail your tickets with subject line "AIRPORT_NAME" or "TRAIN STATION_NAME" PICK UP PLEASE.

  • For guests put up in hotels in the city, the pick up will start approximately an hour before the first ceremony everyday

  • A couple of changing rooms are arranged at the wedding venue. Guests staying in city hotels may bring along their clothes to the venue in the morning & keep changing during the day.

  • Bookings have generally been arranged from 9/12 till 11/12. Check out times depend on your hotel. You may book uber or ola according to your convenience to go back to the airport.
