Indian weddings are celebrated with a lot of enthusiasm and exuberance. They are strongly rooted in culture and tradition and are also very grand and colorful. Authentic Marwari weddings are a throwback to the ages of the Vedas and the great Indian sears who described the rituals in the Vedas. These rituals are still very relevant to the Indian context as there is a deep philosophical and scientific logic behind each of them. Wedding is an elaborate, regal and vibrant affair.
