Wedding Overview


Weddings in India vary regionally, the religion and per personal preferences of the bride and groom. They are festive occasions in India, and in most cases celebrated with extensive decorations, colors, music, dance, costumes and rituals that depend on the religion of the bride and the groom, as well as their preferences. India celebrates about 10 million weddings per year, of which about 80% are Hindu weddings.

Marwari weddings are celebrated with a lot of enthusiasm and exuberance. They are strongly rooted in culture and tradition and are also very grand and colorful. Authentic Marwari weddings are a throwback to the ages of the Vedas and the great Indian sears who described the rituals in the Vedas. These rituals are still very relevant to the Indian context as there is a deep philosophical and scientific logic behind each of them. Wedding is an elaborate, regal and vibrant affair.
