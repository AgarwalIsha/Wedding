Indian Wedding


Indian weddings vary regionally, the religion and per personal preferences of the bride and groom. They are festive occasions in India, and in most cases celebrated with extensive decorations, colors, music, dance, costumes and rituals.

Marwari weddings are strongly rooted in culture and tradition and are a throwback to the ages of the Vedas and the great Indian sears who described the rituals in the Vedas. These rituals have deep philosophical and scientific logic behind them. Indian wedding is an elaborate, regal and vibrant affair.
