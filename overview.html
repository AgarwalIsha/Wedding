Indian Wedding


"He felt now that he was not simply close to her, but that he did not know where he ended and she began. "- Leo Tolstoy

Indian weddings are strongly rooted in culture and tradition and are a throwback to the ages of the Vedas and the great Indian sears who described the rituals in the Vedas. These rituals have deep philosophical and scientific logic behind them. Indian wedding is an elaborate, regal and vibrant affair.
