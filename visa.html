Visa Info

Visitors to India can either apply for a regular visa or an e-Visa. E-Visa is hassle-free to obtain, but it's valid for a shorter duration. Under this scheme, visitors can apply for an Electronic Travel Authorization online, and get visa stamp for entry into the country upon arrival, valid for 60 days & up to three entries.
