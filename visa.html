Visa Info

Visitors to India can either apply for a regular visa or an e-Visa. The e-Visa is hassle-free to obtain, although it's valid for a shorter duration. Under this scheme, visitors can easily apply for an Electronic Travel Authorization online, and then get a visa stamp for entry into the country upon arrival, valid for 60 days & up to three entries.
