Other Important Information


Things to take:

  • Mosquito repellent cream
  • Toilet Papers
  • Packet of emergency travel medicines
  • Some German bread and cheese in case of emergency
  • Open footwear
  • Hand sanitizer

Exchanging money:

If you want, you may get in touch with Anant for Indian currency or use DKB card. Other means of exchanging money may prove expensive.


Dos and Don'ts

  • Public display of affection is not considered positive in India
  • Vehicles drive on left in India. It is advisable to rent a car with driver if you don't have prior driving experience in India
